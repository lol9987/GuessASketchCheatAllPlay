How to use this cheat efficiently

  • 1. Select the length of the word with the slider.
  • 2. Enter the hints in the textboxes as they appear in the game.
  • 3. Words that match the hints and word length will appear in the word list.
  • 4. Try all the words in the list and you'll end up guessing what it is.
    You can also click on the word to copy it to the clipboard.
  • 5. If you need to guess multiple words, move the slider to the end.
    Giving hints would have been slower in this case.
  • 6. Keep in mind that not all words are known, so it might not be there.
    In this case, I invite you to send me feedback.
